About us

Our Mission

Delphi is a social enterprise dedicated to improving the lives of women around the world. Our guiding belief is that while women are often leaders within their communities, they typically lack the resources to scale their impact. Delphi is a centralized resource for women to access information, services, and funding for their personal lives and professional needs.

Our name is inspired by the Oracle of Delphi, one of the most important figures in ancient Greece. Rather than simply seeking to equality for women, Delphi aims to reestablish women as key players as shapers of our shared future.

Membership fees are collected to ensure long term sustainability and greater autonomy than can be achieved as a 501(c)3 or similar entity.

Delphi Programs

Our three leading programs, Helping Hands, Voices, and Discovered Leaders offer a wide range of services including real time advice from medical professionals, grant opportunities, and the ability to share your story.

  • Helping Hands

    Whether you are dealing with a sensitive personal problems or starting a new business, you should not have to feel like you are alone. Members can take advantage of a trained counselor and free business services.

  • Voices

    There is a tremendous amount of knowledge within the Delphi network. Make connections with like minded women from around the world to share your story and hear others.

  • Discovered Leaders

    Women are already in leadership positions but often undervalued by their community and environment. Discovered Leaders seeks to identify those women and provide them with a grant to help them take their ideas to the next level.
